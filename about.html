Who We Are

A Legacy of Excellence in Tech Education

Founded in 1993, DICS has grown from a small computer training center to Delhi's most trusted IT education institution. We've trained over 10,000 students, placed them in top MNCs, startups, and government roles.

Our mission is simple: make quality tech education accessible to everyone. Whether you're a fresh graduate, a working professional looking to upskill, or someone switching careers — we have a program for you.
