FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

Do you offer EMI or installment payment options?+
Yes! We offer 0% EMI options on all courses. You can pay in 3, 6, or 12 monthly installments. Contact our admissions team for details.
Are the courses available online or offline?+
We offer both options — classroom training at our Delhi center and live online classes via Zoom/Google Meet. You can choose based on your preference.
Will I get a certificate after completing the course?+
Yes, all students receive a globally recognized completion certificate. We also help with industry certifications like Google, Microsoft, and AWS.
Is placement assistance guaranteed?+
We provide 100% placement assistance — resume building, mock interviews, and job referrals. Our placement rate is 95% within 3 months of course completion.
How can I enroll for a free demo class?+
Simply fill the contact form above or WhatsApp us. A counsellor will call you within 2 hours to schedule your free demo session.
